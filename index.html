TestPilot-AI Evidence Trend

This dashboard visualizes the evidence_score over time. The score represents a Bayesian posterior estimate of how likely the self-healing locator updates are correct, based on repeated successful runtime fallbacks.

What does evidence_score mean?

Each point is derived from a Beta-Binomial model where:

  • Success = a fallback selector works at runtime
  • Failure = alternative selectors dominate
The resulting posterior mean (0–1) summarizes confidence that the promoted selector is stable. Higher values indicate stronger empirical support from real executions.
